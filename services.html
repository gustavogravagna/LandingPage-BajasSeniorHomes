Who Are We?

Generations Healthcare is proud to be at the forefront of patient care and treatment. Through skilled nursing and behavioral health programs, our facility teams specialize in getting patients back on their feet and back into their homes. In cases where home is not the final goal, our memory care and assisted living services ease our residents’ conditions and improve their quality of life. We offer comprehensive programs of therapy services, including physical, occupational, and speech therapy, as well as intensive stroke and orthopedic rehabilitation, and have achieved impressive outcomes as a result of our state-of-the-art programs and equipment. Our highly trained and reputable staff enjoy their work, and it shows.

Rehabilitation

Some of the most exciting things we do take place every day in our rehabilitation therapy centers. Our in-house therapists and nursing staff work as partners to treat patients who may
have lost strength and mobility due to illness, trauma, or surgery. It is easy to imagine the delight of both the therapist and the patient when a patient who may have been incapacitated in one way or another becomes increasingly independent through concentrated effort and care.
Our therapy departments are led by the rehabilitation director, who supervises a staff of other physical, occupational, and speech therapists. These highly trained and certified therapists work exclusively for Baja Senior Homes.
